Everything was going fine for the civilisation for hundereds of years... until The Great Rupture...

Wars broke out and technology was destroyed and lost. Amidst the chaos, multiple civilisations targetted Apeiropolis, they attempted to wipe out their technology, but Apeiropolis did not back down, they too went to war.

Sadly, Apeiropolis suffered heavily lost and their city was on the brink of distruction, too much was lost already. In a final attempt to save their people and city, a leading net runner, Abantes made a break through.

He figured out how to upload his psyche onto the net, in order to save the city he uploaded the whole civilisation onto the net, the undying monsters, also known as 'Ghosts', became AI guardians whos sole purpose was to protect the city. The ancients sealed off and hid the city, they had swore to protect it and that no one shall lay their hands on it ever again.
